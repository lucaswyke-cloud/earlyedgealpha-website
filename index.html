What the signal looks like

Every coin tells you
exactly where it stands

Hover any highlighted coin and EarlyEdgeAlpha surfaces the full intelligence layer — not just a colour, but the precise data behind it. Source, strength, freshness, trajectory.

In the screenshot, Earendel is flagged HOT via a manual hover trigger, with 6 tweets sampled at a 25.8s average gap and data just 16 seconds old — a real edge, in real time.

  • Source: HOVER or TREND — so you always know whether you triggered the measurement or the engine found it autonomously.
  • Tweet gap: The average time between posts. Compression is the signal — the tighter the gap, the hotter the narrative.
  • Freshness: Data age shown in seconds. Stale data costs you entries. EarlyEdgeAlpha keeps each tracked coin rechecked and current.
  • Continuous coverage: New pairs and coins in their final stretch of bonding receive automatic rechecks — so every coin stays as informed and fresh as possible, page by page.
